Welcome

TO MY TEACHING PORTFOLIO

Welcome to my teaching portfolio! My name is Bahara Baghkhanian and I am currently in the process of earning my Multiple Subject Teaching Credential (June 2018) and Master of Arts in Teaching (August 2018) from the University of California, Irvine.

As an educator, I strive to create an inclusive, equitable learning environment for students to thrive in. By consistently nurturing the potential of all children, I hope to contribute to the development of ethical, insightful, and well-rounded learners who are prepared to meet the challenges of a constantly evolving world.

Teaching Philosophy

I believe that the purpose of education is to provide children with enriching learning opportunities that allow for them to grow as intellectual, social, emotional, and personal beings. It is my priority to create and maintain an inclusive, equitable learning environment for students to thrive in. By nurturing the potential and abilities of each child, while also differentiating instruction accordingly, I aim to ensure that my students feel valued and supported as learners.

As an educator, I believe that student contributions are essential to the functioning of a productive classroom environment. In particular, meaningful learning takes place through active collaboration within the classroom. By designing rich, challenging lessons and learning tasks that promote communication amongst peers, students will have the opportunity to co-construct knowledge, understand diverse perspectives, and combine their unique strengths in purposeful manners.

My ultimate goal as an educator is to not only enhance the knowledge and skills of students, but to also guide them in becoming empowered, productive citizens of society who embrace diversity, and lead ethically responsible lives.
